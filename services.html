ULTRASOUND CLEANSING

Posted by Admin

Face ultrasound cleansing is a cosmetic treatment that uses high-frequency sound waves to deep clean and exfoliate the skin. During the procedure, a device called an ultrasonic spatula is used to produce vibrations that create tiny bubbles in a liquid cleanser. These bubbles help to loosen and remove dirt, oil, and dead skin cells from the surface of the skin.

The ultrasonic spatula also helps to increase circulation, promote collagen production, and enhance the penetration of skincare products into the skin. This can lead to a brighter, smoother, and more youthful-looking complexion. Face ultrasound cleansing is generally considered safe for most skin types, but it may not be suitable for individuals with certain medical conditions or skin sensitivities. It is typically performed by licensed estheticians or skincare professionals in a spa or clinical setting.

OXYGEN BEAUTY THERAPY

Posted by Admin

Oxygen beauty therapy, also known as oxygen facial, is a non-invasive cosmetic treatment that is designed to rejuvenate and revitalize the skin. The therapy involves the use of pressurized oxygen, along with various vitamins, minerals, and other nutrients, to nourish the skin and improve its overall appearance.

During an oxygen facial, a trained professional will apply a specialized serum to the skin, which contains a high concentration of oxygen and other beneficial ingredients. The serum is then applied to the face using a wand or a similar device, which delivers the oxygen and nutrients to the skin through a gentle mist.

The benefits of oxygen beauty therapy include improved skin hydration, increased skin firmness and elasticity, reduced fine lines and wrinkles, and improved overall skin texture and tone. The therapy can also help to reduce redness and inflammation, and it can be an effective treatment for acne-prone skin.

Oxygen beauty therapy is a safe and effective treatment for most skin types, and it can be customized to address specific skin concerns. It is also a non-invasive treatment, meaning that there is no downtime or recovery period required after the procedure. Overall, oxygen beauty therapy is a popular cosmetic treatment that can help to rejuvenate and revitalize the skin, leaving it looking and feeling healthier and more youthful.

DEEP FACE CLEANSING WITH ACIDS

Posted by Admin

Deep facial cleansing with acids is a popular skincare treatment that involves the use of chemical acids to exfoliate and deeply clean the skin. There are several types of acids used in this type of treatment, including alpha hydroxy acids (AHAs) and beta hydroxy acids (BHAs).

AHAs, such as glycolic acid and lactic acid, work by exfoliating the outermost layer of the skin, which helps to remove dead skin cells and improve skin texture. They can also help to reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles, and improve the overall tone and clarity of the skin.

BHAs, such as salicylic acid, work by penetrating deep into the pores, where they dissolve the buildup of dirt, oil, and dead skin cells that can lead to acne breakouts. They also help to reduce inflammation and redness associated with acne.

During a deep facial cleansing with acids treatment, a trained professional will apply a chemical acid solution to the face, which is then left on for a specific amount of time. The acid is then neutralized and removed, leaving the skin feeling smoother, softer, and more refreshed.

It is important to note that deep facial cleansing with acids can be harsh on the skin, and may not be suitable for everyone. Individuals with sensitive skin, eczema, or rosacea may not be good candidates for this type of treatment. It is always recommended to consult with a qualified skincare professional before undergoing any type of facial cleansing treatment.

Darsonvalization

Posted by Admin

Darsonvalization is a cosmetic and therapeutic treatment that uses a high-frequency electrical current to stimulate the skin and underlying tissues. It is named after the French physicist Jacques-Arsène d'Arsonval, who developed the technology in the late 19th century.

During Darsonvalization, a small handheld device called a "Darsonval" or "high-frequency wand" is used to generate an electrical current that is applied to the skin. The wand is fitted with a glass electrode, which is placed on the skin and moved in circular or sweeping motions across the treatment area.

The electrical current generated by the wand produces a mild, tingling sensation on the skin. This sensation is thought to help increase circulation and stimulate the production of collagen and elastin, which can improve skin tone and elasticity.

Darsonvalization is also sometimes used as a therapeutic treatment for conditions such as acne, eczema, and psoriasis. The electrical current can help to reduce inflammation and kill bacteria on the skin, which can help to clear up acne and other skin conditions.

Darsonvalization is generally considered safe when performed by a qualified professional. However, it may not be suitable for everyone, particularly individuals with certain medical conditions or those who are pregnant. It is always recommended to consult with a qualified healthcare provider before undergoing any type of medical or cosmetic treatment.

Led Phototherapy skin mask

Posted by Admin

LED therapy is a non-invasive, painless cosmetic treatment that uses light-emitting diodes (LEDs) to stimulate the skin and promote healing. The therapy is also known as photobiomodulation, or low-level light therapy.

During LED therapy, a device that emits different wavelengths of light is placed over the face, neck, or other areas of the body. The device emits specific wavelengths of light that penetrate the skin at different depths, stimulating cellular activity and promoting healing.

Different colors of LED lights have different benefits for the skin. Red LED lights are commonly used to promote collagen production and improve skin texture, while blue LED lights are used to kill acne-causing bacteria and reduce inflammation.

LED therapy is a painless and non-invasive treatment that does not require any downtime. The therapy can be used to treat a variety of skin concerns, including fine lines and wrinkles, acne, sun damage, and uneven skin tone.

LED therapy is generally considered safe for most people. However, individuals with certain medical conditions or those taking certain medications may not be good candidates for the treatment. It is always recommended to consult with a qualified skincare professional before undergoing any type of cosmetic treatment.
